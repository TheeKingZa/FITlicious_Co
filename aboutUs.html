About THEBACKYARD

Bringing authentic township flavours to Parys since day one.

Who We Are

THEBACKYARD is a local Kasi food spot located in Tumahole, Parys. We specialize in authentic South African township meals — grilled chicken, braai meat, cooked meals, platters, and iconic Kotas.

Our Mission

To serve fresh, flavorful Kasi food that brings people together. We believe every meal should feel like home — made with love and the unique flavours of the township.

Our Values

  • Fresh Ingredients: We use only the best local produce and meat.
  • Community First: Serving the people of Parys with pride and care.
  • Authenticity: Real Kasi taste, prepared the traditional way.
  • Affordability: Great food at fair prices for everyone.
